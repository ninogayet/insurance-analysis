# Insurance Cost Analysis

Analysis of health insurance charges based on patient data (age, BMI, smoking status, region), built with Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib.

## What this project covers
- Distribution of insurance charges across 1338 patients
- Impact of smoking status on charges — smokers pay on average 4x more
- Correlation between age and insurance charges
- Segmentation of risk profiles by smoking status and age

## Key findings
- Smoking is the strongest predictor of insurance cost — average charges of ~32,000 USD vs ~8,000 USD for non-smokers
- Three distinct risk segments visible when plotting age vs charges, explained by smoking status
- Charges increase with age across all risk segments

## Dataset
Medical Cost Personal Dataset — available on Kaggle

## Tools
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib
